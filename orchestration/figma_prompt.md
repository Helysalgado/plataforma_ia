Design a modern SaaS-style academic collaborative platform UI for sharing AI resources in bioinformatics and genetics.

The platform must feel:

-   Professional
    
-   Academic
    
-   Trustworthy
    
-   Clean
    
-   Community-driven
    
-   Modern but not playful
    
-   Minimal and structured
    

Avoid startup flashy style. Keep it institutional and elegant.

Clear hierarchy:

-   Large bold headings
    
-   Medium section titles
    
-   Clean body text
    

Use generous white space.  
Use soft shadows.
